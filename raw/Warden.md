# 🔹 Concept: Warden Robot

💡 **Concept**

The Warden Robot is designed to be the terrestrial guardian and heavy-duty explorer of the Divine World ecosystem. While the Creaking specializes in stealth and perception and the Elder Guardian masters amphibious environments, the Warden focuses on resilience, environmental awareness, and operation in harsh conditions.

Inspired by the massive frame of the Minecraft Warden and the durability of an Iron Golem, this robot is intended to survive situations that would challenge other Divine World robots. Rather than relying solely on vision, the Warden is built around alternative perception systems, allowing it to function effectively in darkness, dust, fog, caves, and other low-visibility environments.

Its perception system draws inspiration from bats and the in-game Warden itself. Using ultrasonic echolocation, the robot can construct a three-dimensional understanding of its surroundings even when cameras become ineffective. During daylight or in well-lit environments, traditional cameras supplement the sonar system to provide richer environmental information.

The Warden's body is heavily reinforced with impact-resistant materials and cushioning systems that allow it to withstand falls, collisions, and rough terrain. Instead of carefully scripted locomotion, the robot must learn how to balance, walk, climb obstacles, and recover from mistakes through experience.

The ultimate goal is to create a durable autonomous platform capable of operating where other robots may struggle, making it the ideal protector, explorer, and resilience-testing platform within the Divine World ecosystem.
